\vskip .1in 
(1) \, Write down an explicit formula for a function $u$ solving the initial value problem 
$$\cases u_t + b \cdot \nabla u + c \, u =\, 0 \qquad \IR^n \times (0,\infty) \\
\qquad \qquad \quad \quad u =\, g \qquad \IR^n \times \{t=0\} \endcases $$

where $c \in \IR$, $b \in \IR^n$ and $g : \IR^n \to \IR$ are given.
 \vskip .2in
 
 (2) \, Let $f$ be a continuous function on an open set $D \subset \IR^n$ such that 
 $$ \int_{D_0}  \, f(x) \, dx \, =\, 0 \qquad \quad \text{ for all }\quad  D_0 \subset D.$$
 
Prove that then $f \equiv 0$ on $D$.
