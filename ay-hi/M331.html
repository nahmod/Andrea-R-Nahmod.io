Syllabus for the Course (click). This course in an introduction to ordinary differential equations. 

We will cover most of Ch. 1, 2, 4 and 6.  The topics include:

  • First order equations
  • Second order linear equations
  • Systems of linear differential equations.
  • Forcing and Resonances.
  • Laplace transform methods.
