  •  Description:
This is a course on differential and integral calculus of several variables from a more advance perspective than Math 233.
The main focus will be on integration over regions, paths and surfaces; the change of variables formulae and the integral
theorems of Green, Gauss and Stokes.
Special Note:
Knowledge of  Chapters 1 through 4 of Marsden and Tromba is expected. We will formally start
in Chapter 4 after a quick review of some concepts in Chapters 1 and 2.  But note that Chapters 1, 2 and 3
will be essentially assumed.  Please do the Review homework below for these sections!!
